<p><ul><li>Rigid Couplings</li><li>Flexible Couplings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive coupling market is primarily divided into two types: rigid couplings and flexible couplings. Rigid couplings provide a solid connection between shafts, ensuring precise alignment and torque transmission, often used in applications requiring minimal movement. In contrast, flexible couplings accommodate misalignment, vibrations, and axial movement, which enhances performance and durability in dynamic conditions. These couplings are essential for transmitting power efficiently in automotive systems, contributing to improved vehicle performance and reliability.</p></p>
