<p><strong>The Humidity Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relative Humidity Sensor</li><li>Absolute Humidity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The humidity sensor market is primarily categorized into relative humidity sensors and absolute humidity sensors. Relative humidity sensors measure the moisture content in the air as a percentage of the maximum amount of moisture the air can hold at a given temperature. In contrast, absolute humidity sensors quantify the actual mass of water vapor present in a given volume of air, regardless of temperature. Both types are crucial for various applications, including climate control, agriculture, and industrial processes.</p></p>
