<p><ul><li>Water Soluble Flavors</li><li>Oil Soluble Flavors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pet care flavors market includes water-soluble and oil-soluble flavors, each catering to specific product formulations. Water-soluble flavors are primarily used in liquids and wet foods, ensuring uniform distribution and enhancing palatability. They are popular in treats and supplements. In contrast, oil-soluble flavors are ideal for dry food products, as they remain stable during processing and provide lasting taste. Both types play a crucial role in improving the sensory appeal of pet food and treats, influencing pet acceptance and enjoyment.</p></p>
