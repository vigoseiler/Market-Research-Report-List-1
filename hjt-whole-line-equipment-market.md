<p><p>The HJT Whole Line Equipment market encompasses various technologies integral to heterojunction solar cell production. PECVD (Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition) equipment is crucial for depositing thin films of silicon, enhancing cell efficiency. PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) equipment is employed for creating thin metal layers, crucial for electrical contacts. Screen printing equipment is utilized for applying conductive pastes onto solar cells, ensuring effective current collection. Together, these technologies optimize the manufacturing process, boosting performance and lowering production costs in the solar industry.</p></p>
