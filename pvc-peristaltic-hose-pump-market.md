<p><p>The PVC Peristaltic Hose Pump is a type of positive displacement pump that utilizes a flexible hose to transport fluids. The mechanism involves rollers that compress the hose, creating a vacuum that draws fluid into the pump and pushes it along the hose as the rollers move forward. This design minimizes contamination risks and allows for handling a wide range of fluid viscosities, making it suitable for various industries including pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and water treatment.</p><p>The PVC Peristaltic Hose Pump Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.8% during the forecast period, driven by rising demand for efficient and hygienic fluid transfer solutions in diverse applications.
