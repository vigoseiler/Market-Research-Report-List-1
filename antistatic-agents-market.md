<p><ul><li>Cationic Antistic Agent</li><li>Anionic Antistatic Agent</li><li>Non-ionic Antistatic Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antistatic agents market comprises three primary types: cationic, anionic, and non-ionic antistatic agents. Cationic antistatic agents carry a positive charge and are suitable for use on negatively charged surfaces, enhancing conductivity. Anionic antistatic agents have a negative charge and are effective on positively charged materials. Non-ionic antistatic agents, which possess no charge, are versatile and compatible with various substrates. These agents are widely used in plastics, textiles, and electronics to reduce static electricity and improve product performance.</p></p>
