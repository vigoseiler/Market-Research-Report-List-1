<p><p>The beard trimmer market is primarily divided into two types: reciprocating and rotary trimmers. Reciprocating type beard trimmers utilize a back-and-forth motion of the blades, offering precision and the ability to handle thicker hair. They are favored for their efficiency in trimming and shaping. On the other hand, rotary type beard trimmers use circular blades, providing a more versatile cutting action, ideal for longer hair and rounded contours. Each type caters to different grooming preferences and styles, enhancing customer choices in personal care.</p></p>
