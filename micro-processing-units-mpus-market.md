<p><strong>The Micro Processing Units MPUs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Read-Only MPU</li><li>Read / Write MPU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Micro Processing Units (MPUs) are classified into two main market types based on their data handling capabilities. Read-Only MPUs are designed to execute pre-stored programs and cannot modify their content, making them suitable for fixed applications like embedded systems. In contrast, Read/Write MPUs allow both reading and modifying data, providing flexibility for dynamic applications such as personal computers and mobile devices. This differentiation influences their use cases, performance, and design in various technological environments.</p></p>
