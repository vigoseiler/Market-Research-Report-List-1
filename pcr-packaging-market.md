<p><p>The PCR (Post-Consumer Recycled) packaging market includes three primary types: PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), PP (Polypropylene), and HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene). PET is widely used for bottles and containers due to its recyclability and clarity. PP is valued for its strength and versatility, often found in containers and films. HDPE is known for its durability and resistance, commonly used in milk jugs and detergent bottles. Together, these materials enhance sustainability by reducing plastic waste and promoting circular economy practices.</p></p>
