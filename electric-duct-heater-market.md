<p><strong>The Electric Duct Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Coil Duct Heater</li><li>Tubular Duct Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric duct heater market can be categorized into two main types: open coil duct heaters and tubular duct heaters. Open coil duct heaters consist of heating elements that are exposed to airflow, providing efficient and rapid heat transfer. They are often used for high-capacity applications. In contrast, tubular duct heaters feature heating elements enclosed in a metal tube, offering enhanced safety and durability, making them suitable for a range of environments, including commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
