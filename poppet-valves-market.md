<p><p>The poppet valves market is divided into four main types based on actuation methods. Pneumatic type poppet valves use compressed air for operation, ideal for applications requiring swift actuation. Manual type poppet valves are operated through a lever or handwheel, providing user control for various processes. Electric type poppet valves utilize electric actuators, offering precise control and automation in various systems. Hydraulic type poppet valves operate under high-pressure fluids, suitable for heavy machinery and equipment where power is essential.</p></p>
