<p><p>The resuscitators and transport ventilators market encompasses various types, including Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) and Noninvasive Positive Pressure Ventilation (NIPPV). CPAP delivers a constant airflow to keep airways open, commonly used for patients with sleep apnea or respiratory distress. NIPPV provides positive pressure support during both inhalation and exhalation, ideal for managing conditions like COPD. Other devices in this market may include portable ventilators and manual resuscitation bags, designed for emergency and transport settings to support patients in respiratory distress.</p></p>
