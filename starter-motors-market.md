<p><p>The starter motors market comprises three main types: electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic starter motors. Electric starter motors, widely used in automotive applications, convert electrical energy into mechanical power. Pneumatic starter motors utilize compressed air for starting engines, making them suitable for heavy machinery and aviation. Hydraulic starter motors rely on hydraulic fluid pressure to initiate engine operation, often found in large industrial equipment. Each type serves specific industries, emphasizing efficiency and reliability in engine starting mechanisms.</p></p>
