<p><ul><li>CSF Shunts</li><li>CSF Drainage Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF) management devices primarily include CSF shunts and CSF drainage systems. CSF shunts are implanted systems that redirect excess cerebrospinal fluid from the brain to another body compartment, preventing conditions like hydrocephalus. CSF drainage systems, on the other hand, facilitate the external collection of CSF to relieve intracranial pressure. Both types of devices are essential in treating neurological conditions, ensuring patient safety and improving quality of life through effective management of cerebrospinal fluid levels.</p></p>
