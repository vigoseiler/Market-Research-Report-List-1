<p><p>The 3D visual perception products market encompasses several technologies: </p><p>1. **Structured Light** utilizes projected patterns to capture depth information, suitable for applications like facial recognition and object scanning.  </p><p>2. **iToF (Indirect Time of Flight)** measures the time it takes for light to return after reflecting off an object, offering high accuracy in depth sensing.  </p><p>3. **dToF (Direct Time of Flight)** sends short light pulses to measure distance, ideal for outdoor environments.  </p><p>4. **LiDAR** employs laser pulses for high-resolution mapping and object detection.  </p><p>5. **Others** include various emerging technologies and methods enhancing 3D visual perception in diverse applications.</p></p>
