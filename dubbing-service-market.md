<p><strong>The Dubbing Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Synchronised Dubbing</li><li>Oversound Dubbing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dubbing services in the media market primarily include synchronized dubbing and oversound dubbing. Synchronized dubbing involves replacing the original dialogue with translated audio while ensuring lip-sync accuracy, maintaining the visual performance's emotional nuances. In contrast, oversound dubbing layers translated dialogue over the original audio, allowing the original voices to be partially preserved. This technique is often used in documentaries or educational content, where the authenticity of the speaker's voice enhances the viewing experience while providing translation.</p></p>
