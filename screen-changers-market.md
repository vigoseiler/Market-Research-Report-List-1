<p><p>Screen changers are devices used in extrusion processes to filter contaminants from molten polymers. The market is segmented into manual and hydraulic types. Manual screen changers require operators to change screens manually, offering simplicity and lower costs but less efficiency during production runs. In contrast, hydraulic screen changers automate the screen-changing process, allowing for continuous operation and reduced downtime. This results in higher productivity and improved output quality, making hydraulic options preferable for larger-scale industrial applications.</p></p>
