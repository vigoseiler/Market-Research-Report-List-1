<p><ul><li>Platen Blister Packaging Machine</li><li>Rotary Blister Packaging Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blister packaging machine market is primarily categorized into two types: platen and rotary blister packaging machines. Platen blister packaging machines utilize a flat plate mechanism to seal packages, offering precise control over the sealing process, making them ideal for small to medium production runs. In contrast, rotary blister packaging machines operate on a continuous rotary system, enabling higher production speeds and efficiency, making them suitable for large-scale manufacturing. Both types serve a variety of industries, including pharmaceuticals and consumer goods.</p></p>
