<p><strong>The Automotive ABS Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive ABS Sensor market primarily includes Hall Effect and Magnetic Electric types. Hall Effect sensors use a semiconductor to detect changes in magnetic fields, providing precise speed and position data for anti-lock braking systems. In contrast, Magnetic Electric sensors rely on the variation of magnetic fields generated by wheel movement, offering reliable performance at lower costs. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle safety and stability by preventing wheel lock-up during braking, contributing to overall driving performance.</p></p>
