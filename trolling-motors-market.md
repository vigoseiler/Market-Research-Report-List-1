<p><ul><li>Bow Mount</li><li>Transom Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The trolling motor market is primarily divided into two types: bow mount and transom mount. Bow mount trolling motors are designed for installation at the front of the boat, offering enhanced maneuverability and control, ideal for fishing in tight spots. In contrast, transom mount trolling motors are attached at the back, providing straightforward operation and are suitable for various boat sizes. Both types cater to different fishing styles and preferences, making them essential for anglers seeking optimal performance on the water.</p></p>
