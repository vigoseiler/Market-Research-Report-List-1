<p><p>The Marine Windows market is categorized into three main types: Fixed, Sliding, and Hinged Windows. Fixed windows provide a solid, unmovable structure that offers unobstructed views and high strength, making them ideal for high-pressure marine environments. Sliding windows offer flexibility and ease of use, allowing for ventilation while maintaining a sleek profile. Hinged windows enable full access to openings, facilitating maintenance and cleaning, while also ensuring security and durability against harsh weather conditions at sea.</p></p>
