<p><p>The low migration ink market includes four main printing technologies: gravure, flexography, offset, and digital. Gravure printing utilizes engraved cylinders for high-quality, continuous patterns, making it suitable for large runs. Flexography employs flexible plates, ideal for printing on various substrates, especially packaging. Offset printing transfers ink from a plate to a rubber blanket, then to the substrate, ideal for high-quality commercial printing. Digital printing directly transfers images from digital files to substrates, offering flexibility and quick turnaround for short runs and personalized content.</p></p>
