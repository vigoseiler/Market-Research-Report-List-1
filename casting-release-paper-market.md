<p><p>The Casting & Release Paper market comprises two primary types: Release Paper and Casting Paper. Release paper is designed for easy separation from adhesives or coatings, commonly used in medical, adhesive, and industrial applications. It ensures smooth peeling without compromising the quality of the underlying material. In contrast, casting paper is utilized in the production of synthetic leather and other textured surfaces, providing a moldable base for various materials. Both types are essential in enhancing product functionality and efficiency across multiple industries.</p></p>
