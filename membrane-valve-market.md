<p><p>Membrane valves are precision control devices used in various applications to regulate the flow of fluids. They consist of a flexible membrane, typically made of elastomeric materials, that acts as a barrier to separate the fluid from the actuator mechanism. This type of valve is particularly valued in industries where hygiene and contamination control are critical, such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and biotechnology.</p><p>The Membrane Valve Market is witnessing significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 13.9% during the forecast period.
