<p><p>Speed limit radar signs are categorized by their power sources: battery-powered, AC-powered, and solar-powered. Battery-powered signs are portable and ideal for temporary use, offering flexibility in deployment. AC-powered signs provide continuous operation but require a connection to the electrical grid, making them suitable for fixed locations. Solar-powered signs harness sunlight for energy, promoting sustainability and reducing operational costs, especially in remote areas. Each type addresses specific needs in traffic management by balancing power availability, installation, and maintenance considerations.</p></p>
