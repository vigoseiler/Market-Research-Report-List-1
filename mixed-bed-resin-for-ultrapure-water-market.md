<p><p>The mixed bed resin market for ultrapure water comprises two primary types: gel-gel type and gel-macroporous type. Gel-gel type resins consist of uniform gel structure beads that effectively remove ion contaminants, ensuring high purity levels. In contrast, gel-macroporous type resins feature a combination of gel and macroporous structures, providing enhanced exchange capacities and faster kinetics, making them suitable for applications requiring rapid purification. Both types are crucial in industries like pharmaceuticals, microelectronics, and power generation, where ultrapure water is essential.</p></p>
