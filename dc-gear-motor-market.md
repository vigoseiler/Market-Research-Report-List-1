<p><p>The DC gear-motor market is primarily divided into single-phase and three-phase types. Single-phase gear motors are commonly used in residential and light industrial applications, offering simplicity and ease of installation. They operate on standard AC power and require minimal maintenance. In contrast, three-phase gear motors are designed for more demanding industrial environments, providing higher efficiency, better torque, and reduced energy consumption. They are suitable for heavy-duty applications, making them a preferred choice for large-scale manufacturing and automation processes.</p></p>
