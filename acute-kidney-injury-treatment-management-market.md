<p><strong>The Acute Kidney Injury Treatment & Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prerenal Acute Kidney Injury</li><li>Intrinsic Renal Acute Kidney Injury</li><li>Postrenal Acute Kidney Injury</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Acute Kidney Injury (AKI) treatment and management market is segmented into three main types: prerenal, intrinsic renal, and postrenal acute kidney injury. Prerenal AKI is often caused by reduced blood flow to the kidneys and may require fluid resuscitation and addressing the underlying cause. Intrinsic renal AKI results from direct damage to kidney tissue, necessitating medications and supportive therapies. Postrenal AKI arises from obstructed urine flow, typically managed through relieving the obstruction and restoring kidney function. Each type requires tailored strategies for effective treatment.</p></p>
