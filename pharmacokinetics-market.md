<p><strong>The Pharmacokinetics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Noncompartmental Methods</li><li>Compartmental Methods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmacokinetics market is divided into two main types: noncompartmental and compartmental methods. Noncompartmental methods focus on direct analysis of drug concentration over time without assuming a specific distribution model, making them simpler and faster for evaluating drug parameters. Compartmental methods, on the other hand, involve dividing the body into compartments to analyze drug distribution and elimination more intricately, allowing for detailed modeling of pharmacokinetic behavior. Both methods are crucial for drug development and regulatory submissions.</p></p>
