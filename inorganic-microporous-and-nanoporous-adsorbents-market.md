Clays, such as bentonite, offer natural adsorption and swelling characteristics. Activated alumina is a highly porous form of aluminum oxide, effective in removing moisture and contaminants. Silica gel, a porous form of silica, is widely used for desiccation and adsorption. The "Other" category includes materials like metal-organic frameworks (MOFs) and activated carbons, each serving specialized applications.</p></p>
