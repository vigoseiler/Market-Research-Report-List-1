<p><p>The beer dispense systems market comprises various types to cater to different serving needs. The Direct Draw System delivers beer directly from kegs to the tap, ideal for short distances. The Air Cooled System utilizes fans to circulate cold air around the beer lines, maintaining temperature over moderate distances. The Glycol Cooled System employs a chilled glycol solution, providing optimal temperature control for long-distance dispense, making it suitable for large venues. Each system offers unique benefits based on application requirements.</p></p>
