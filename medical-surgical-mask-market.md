<p><strong>The Medical Surgical Mask Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Woven Medical Surgical Masks</li><li>Non-woven Medical Surgical Masks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical surgical mask market is primarily divided into two types: woven and non-woven masks. Woven medical surgical masks are made from interlaced fibers, offering durability and breathability but may have lower filtration efficiency. In contrast, non-woven medical surgical masks are crafted from synthetic fibers using processes like melt-blown technology, providing superior filtration capabilities and fluid resistance. Non-woven masks are generally preferred in clinical settings due to their effectiveness in blocking pathogens and ensuring user safety.</p></p>
