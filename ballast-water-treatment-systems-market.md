<p><p>The Ballast Water Treatment Systems market is primarily divided into two types: chemical methods and physical methods. Chemical methods involve the use of biocides or other chemicals to kill harmful organisms in ballast water, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations. Conversely, physical methods utilize techniques such as filtration, ultraviolet light, or heat to remove or inactivate pathogens without introducing chemicals. Both methods aim to minimize the ecological impact of ballast water discharge while adhering to international maritime standards for environmental protection.</p></p>
