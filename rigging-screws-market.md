<p><strong>The Rigging Screws Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Body</li><li>Closed-Body</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rigging screws market is divided into two main types: open-body and closed-body. Open-body rigging screws feature a visible, exposed thread design allowing for easy adjustments and quicker rigging, ideal for applications requiring frequent tension adjustments. In contrast, closed-body rigging screws have a fully enclosed design, providing added protection against debris and environmental factors, making them suitable for more permanent rigging setups. Both types serve essential roles in securing and tensioning rigging systems in various industries.</p></p>
