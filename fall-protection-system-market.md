<p><p>The fall protection system market encompasses various types designed to ensure worker safety at heights. Harnesses secure the worker's body, distributing fall forces across the shoulders and thighs. Lanyards connect the harness to an anchor point, allowing mobility while preventing falls. Self-retracting lifelines use a spring mechanism to automatically adjust as the worker moves, providing constant tension. Belts offer support but are typically used in specific contexts. Other market types may include anchorage devices, guardrails, and protective nets, catering to diverse safety needs.</p></p>
