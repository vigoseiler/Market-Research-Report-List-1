<p><p>The amorphous steel market is primarily segmented into two types: amorphous ribbon and nano-crystalline ribbon. Amorphous ribbons are thin, non-crystalline metallic strips known for their excellent magnetic properties and high strength, making them suitable for electronic applications like transformers and sensors. In contrast, nano-crystalline ribbons, which incorporate nanostructured crystalline phases, offer enhanced mechanical properties and magnetic performance, often used in advanced technology applications. Both types are pivotal in industries focusing on energy efficiency and high-performance materials.</p></p>
